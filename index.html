Lands Between

The Lands Between is a vast domain ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal and the setting of Elden Ring. It is blessed by the Elden Ring and the Erdtree, and so too are many of the people throughout the lands. constituent lands include six vast areas each ruled by a demigod figure.

Tarnished

The Tarnished are individuals who lost the grace of the Erdtree and were consequently banished from the Lands Between by Queen Marika the Eternal.The Tarnished were beckoned by lost grace, fragmentary divine light from the Erdtree, to return.

Enemy

Bosses in Elden Ring are powerful enemies that add challenging experiences to the game. Bosses are encountered throughout the game in both the overworld and inside traditional dungeon-style levels. While some must be fought in order to progress through the story, most are optional.
